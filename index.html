What this tool does

PCEC WH SQFT Forecast converts a D-Chart unit-forecast file (Net Sales + Inventory, by product, by month) into projected warehouse floor-space (SQFT) needs at each of the three regional warehouses — FONTANA, Savannah, and Aurora — plus a combined Total SQFT view.

Calculation logic (per product, per month)

  1. Effective units — Inventory units are combined with a portion of forecasted Net Sales (the "MWO" buffer), using a fixed MWO rate (currently 15%):
    effective units = Inventory + (Net Sales × MWO_RATE)
  2. Raw SQFT — Each product has a CFT-per-unit (cubic feet per unit) constant. Units are converted to cubic feet and then halved (an empirical packing/stacking factor) to get raw SQFT:
    raw SQFT = (effective units × CFT per unit) / 2
  3. Warehouse allocation % — Each product code has a fixed split (editable under ⚙ Allocations) describing what % of its volume historically/operationally goes to Aurora, FONTANA, and Savannah. These three percentages always sum to 100% per product.
  4. Warehouse-specific multiplier — Each warehouse view then applies its own multiplier (editable under 🧮 Factors) on top of the allocated SQFT — e.g. a "COI factor" for FONTANA, and separate factors for Savannah and Aurora — to account for stacking/clearance differences at that facility:
    warehouse SQFT = raw SQFT × allocation % × warehouse factor
  5. Totals & capacity — Per-product SQFT is summed into a monthly total per warehouse. That total is then compared against the Max SQFT capacity set for that warehouse (under 📦 Capacity) to flag months that are near or over capacity (warning/critical thresholds).
  6. MWO overstack — For the main bulk product (code FA000), an additional 33% "overstack" line is shown separately to flag temporary surplus inventory exposure.

Other tabs

  • Total SQFT — Combined, unallocated SQFT need before any warehouse split is applied.
  • FONTANA / Savannah / Aurora — SQFT need at that specific warehouse, after allocation % and warehouse factor are applied.
  • 📈 Forecast Variance — Compares the same target month's forecast across multiple uploaded snapshots (archived D-Chart files) to show how the forecast for that month has drifted over time.

Editable inputs

  • ⚙ Allocations — % split of each product's volume across Aurora / FONTANA / Savannah (must total 100% per row).
  • 🧮 Factors — Multipliers applied per warehouse (FONTANA COI factor, Savannah factor, Aurora factor) when converting allocated SQFT into final warehouse SQFT.
  • 📦 Capacity — Max SQFT for each warehouse, used to color-code the table/chart (normal / warning / critical).

All calculations run client-side in your browser — no data is sent to a server. Changing any allocation, factor, or capacity input immediately recalculates every tab.
